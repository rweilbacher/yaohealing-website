01 — Soul Sessions

Soul Sessions

For those who are ready to meet themselves — raw, honest, and with lots of gentleness.

Hello, I'm Manisha and I will meet you in your present truth, even when it might feel uncomfortable.

I won't look away from the parts you've learned to hide. Not the messy ones, not the contradictory ones, not the ones you're still trying to understand. And also not the bright and shiny parts, that you are.

Together, we create a space where everything in you is allowed, brought to light, held and seen mindfully and wholesomely.

This is a space for realness. Not for fixing. Not for becoming someone else. But for coming into deeper contact with who you already are — underneath the patterns, the roles, the protection.

I work intuitively and in deep attunement with you and the cosmos. There is no fixed structure, because you are not something to be put into one. Instead, I listen deeply — to you, your body, your energy, and what wants to unfold.

02 — Possibilities

What this work
can open

  • Being deeply seen in parts you usually hide
  • Moving through emotional blocks or inner tension
  • Reconnecting with your body and inner knowing
  • Clarity in relationships, decisions, or direction
  • Softening control and allowing what is there
  • Accessing deeper states of awareness and truth

Our sessions can be gentle. And it can be confronting. Often, it is both.

03 — Process

How we work

Each session is unique. We follow what is present and alive, not a plan.

Honest, present-moment dialogue (circling)
Systemic coaching
Conscious connected breathwork
Meditation & silence
Tea ceremony
Somatic awareness

Constellation work
Sound healing
Creative expression (voice, art, music)
Plant medicines
And what wants to emerge

I don't choose but respond to you and your needs. If you feel a particular calling to a certain modality, you are welcome to name that at the beginning of our session.

Session Flow

Each session unfolds in its own timing. We usually spend between 60 and 100 minutes together, depending on what is present and needed.

Energy Exchange

I offer these sessions within a range and we find a price together that feels aligned for both of us. For orientation, sessions usually fall between €100–150.
